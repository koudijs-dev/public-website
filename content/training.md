I turn complex technical topics into something teams can understand and act on. My training is practical and grounded in real-world delivery work — no slides-only theory, but the kind of knowledge that changes how a team actually works.

# Public courses

I teach professionally through [ICT Improve](https://ict-improve.nl/), a Dutch training institute for IT professionals. These courses run on a regular schedule and are open for individual enrolment.

* [Delivering Modern Cloud-Native Software](https://ict-improve.nl/training/delivering-modern-cloud-native-software-en): understand better how Kubernetes exactly can deploy your workloads into production. Covers the fundamentals of cloud-native architecture, container orchestration, and modern delivery pipelines. Focus on building a clear mental model rather than just learning commands.

* [Applied AI for Test Automation with Playwright](https://ict-improve.nl/training/applied-ai-for-test-automation-with-playwright-en): using AI to write and maintain browser tests. Covers practical workflows for getting useful output from AI tools, understanding what the generated code actually does, and building a sustainable test automation setup.
